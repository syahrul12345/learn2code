Why Code during the formative years?

As we see the world moves into a digital society, coding skills become ever more important.

Programming requires strong problem solving skills, and it is best to nurture these skills during the formative years. That's why our coding camps are designed to be short and effective - they keep your child engaged, have fun and more importantly develop the required motor skills for them to solve programming challenges that they will face in school.

Programming is also a hollistic activity - it promotes cognitive growth by being both a technical and creative pursuit. There are thousands of ways to solve a programming problem, and your child will definately enjoy it.

Equip your child with these skillsets

Problem Solving skills

Learn to break complex problems into simpler problems and tackle them algorithmatically.

Innovation

Learn how technology is shaping the world, and how they too can make a cool project!

Analytical Creativity

Discover how a single problem can be solved in many creative ways and learn how to analyze them
